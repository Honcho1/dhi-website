About Us

Diverse Health Initiative (DHI), a non-profit organisation, established in 2021, is committed to reducing mortality and morbidity rate by 65% before 2030, focusing on holistic preventive healthcare in hard-to-reach areas. Targeting vulnerable women, girls, children, and Persons With Disabilities (PWDs), our mission aims for inclusivity and empowerment.
